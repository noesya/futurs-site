FUTURs is a modular game combining a colourful, inspirational and actionable identity.

It empowers players to understand the interconnection between climate, biodiversity, social justice and well-being. They are given solutions and actionable steps to lead positive change.

alt: >- credit: >- buttons: - kind: heading title: >- 128 cards to imagine a thriving future position: 1 rank: 2 - kind: block template: chapter title: >- ranks: self: false data: layout: no_background text: >-

With its 128 cards, FUTURs offers citizens an alternative to a desirable, happy, and thriving future for all.

The game empowers citizens through various card games to play, an interactive card directory to learn, as well as workshops to imagine the future they aspire to lead.

CARD GAME
5+ rules to play inclusive games with easy dynamics to have fun while learning. Game modes include individual, team, competitive and cooperative rules.

INTERACTIVE RESOURCE
A directory of the 128 cards to better understand, learn and take action for the future. Articles, podcasts, news articles, reports, art performances, and practical actions.

WORKSHOPS
Activities to use the cards to connect people, foster imagination, and take action. Fit for educators, students, companies, policymakers, and changemakers
